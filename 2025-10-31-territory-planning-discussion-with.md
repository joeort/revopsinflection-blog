Territory planning is a key activity as part of your Annual Operating Plan process. It takes months to do it right and do it well. It incorporates insights from historical segmentation performance to TAM, SOM and TRM.

If you are looking for practical tips on planning territories (and all the steps along the way), check out the [Uncharted Territory Interview](https://unchartedterritory.gradient.works/p/building-great-territories-with-joe) with Gradient Works CEO, Hayes Davis and our very own, Joe Ort.

You’ll hear about:

-   Different types of territory structures - geo, named, checkouts, dynamic books
    
-   All of the pre-work to make territory planning successful
    
-   What you need to make sure decisions are made and progress is moved forward; avoiding constant horse trading.
